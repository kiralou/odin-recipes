Clear Slime

A see-through goo that's clear as glass

This crystal clear slime is thick, moldable, and looks like liquid glass. It's simple to make, and fun to spice up with colors, scents, glitter, and more!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup clear glue (Elmer's is the best, but any clear glue with PVA wil do the trick
  • 1 cup water
  • slime activator*
  • 2 pumps foaming hand soap (must be clear or your foam won't be)
  • Mixing bowl

*when using contact solution as activator, you'll also want to mix 1 Tablespoon of baking soda in 1 cup of warm water. Stir this in with the glue before adding 2 Tablespoons of contact solution

Let's get started

  1. Pour (1) cup of clear glue into mixing bowl
  2. In another bowl, mix (1/2) teaspoon of Borax into (1) cup of warm water until diluted
  3. Slowly stir in the Borax solution with the clear glue with a spoon. It will start taking on a slime-like texture immediately.
  4. Stir until all liquid is absorbed. This can take a few minutes so be patient and keep stirring
